    The Restauruant

    We are your neighborhood award-winning barbecue restaurant in Lexington, KY, where barbecue is done right. With every trip you take to our family-friendly restaurant, you can count on outstanding service, an inviting atmosphere, and, of course, the best-tasting barbecue meals you've ever sunk your teeth into.

    The menu

    What’s for dinner tonight? How about a slab of mouthwatering ribs? Or maybe it’s a hearty serving of barbecue brisket you desire? You could also enjoy a juicy pulled pork sandwich and a hot bowl of chili while your kids chow down on some smoked buffalo wings and ooey-gooey mac-and-cheese. Whatever suits your fancy, your only way to satisfy all these cravings is by taking a quick trip to Red State BBQ.
